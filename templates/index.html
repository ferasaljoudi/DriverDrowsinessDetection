Driver Drowsiness Detection


Model Description


This prediction is powered by a custom-trained convolutional neural network (CNN) model designed to accurately classify states of driver alertness. Key features of the model include:

  • Pre-Trained VGG16 Base: Utilizes the VGG16 architecture as a starting point, leveraging transfer learning for enhanced feature extraction.
  • Custom Top Layers: Additional dense layers are added on top of the VGG16 base to tailor the model to our specific classification tasks.
  • Four-Class Classification: The model distinguishes between four different classes: "Open", "Close", "Yawn", and "No Yawn", aiding in the detection of drowsiness and attentiveness.
  • High Accuracy: Achieved an accuracy of over 90% on the validation set during training, indicating strong predictive performance.

This model represents a significant step forward in the application of artificial intelligence for improving road safety and preventing accidents due to driver fatigue.
